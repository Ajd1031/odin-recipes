Secret sauce

Description

Absolutely delicious sauce that can be eaten with practically anything that is savory! Of course, it is also serves nicely as a dip for tortilla chips

Ingredients

  • 2 cans of media crema
  • Minced garlic
  • Knorrs chicken seasoning
  • Olive oil
  • 2 buschles of cilantro

Directions

  1. Put sauce pan on low heat and add in olive oil
  2. Once the oil has heated up, add in minced garlic and allow it to simmer until fragrant
  3. While the garlic is in the pan, chop of the cilatro as finaly as you can
  4. Add the media crema into the pan
  5. Once you stirred the media crema and oil together, add in the cilantro and 1.5 spoonfuls of the chicken seasoning and stirr until uniform
  6. Once the cilatro has a limp texture add chicken seasoning to taste
  7. Once satisfied with taste, pour the sauce into the serving dish or storage container
  8. Enjoy!
